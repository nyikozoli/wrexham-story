The Takeover

In February 2021, Hollywood stars Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney completed their takeover of Wrexham AFC — the third-oldest professional football club in the world, languishing in the fifth tier of English football.

What began as a seemingly unlikely venture — two A-list actors buying a small Welsh club — quickly became one of the most remarkable stories in football history. With genuine investment, smart recruitment, and an infectious enthusiasm that united an entire town, they set Wrexham on a path that nobody could have predicted.

Three consecutive promotions. Record attendances. A hit documentary series. A club reportedly valued at £100 million. And a community reborn. This is the story of how Wrexham went from non-league obscurity to the Championship — the first team ever to achieve three successive promotions in England's top five divisions.

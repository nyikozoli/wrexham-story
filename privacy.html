Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 March 2026

1. About This Site

The Wrexham Story is an unofficial, non-commercial fan tribute site documenting the journey of Wrexham AFC. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Wrexham AFC, FX Networks, or any other organisation mentioned on the site.

2. What Data We Collect

This site is a static website. We do not:

  • Use cookies (no first-party or third-party cookies)
  • Use analytics or tracking scripts (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, etc.)
  • Collect personal information (no forms, no sign-ups, no email collection)
  • Use local storage or session storage
  • Load resources from third-party servers (fonts are self-hosted)

In short: we collect zero personal data from visitors.

3. Hosting Provider

This site is hosted on a static hosting platform. The hosting provider may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) as part of normal server operations. These logs are processed by the hosting provider under their own privacy policy and are not accessible to us.

4. No Third-Party Data Transfers

This site does not make any requests to external third-party services. All fonts, stylesheets, scripts, and data files are served directly from the same domain. No data about your visit is sent to Google, Facebook, or any other third party by this site.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

Since we do not collect or process any personal data, there is no personal data to access, rectify, or delete. However, under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access — request confirmation of what data (if any) is held about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Restriction — request restriction of processing
  • Portability — request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Object — object to processing of your data

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your data rights, you can reach us via the GitHub repository for this project.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
